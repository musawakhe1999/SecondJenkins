When you open the game, you'll see a grid of tiles with various colors. Each color has a pair. Click on a tile to reveal its color. Click on another tile to reveal its color.
If the colors of the two tiles match, they will remain revealed. If the colors don't match, the tiles will be hidden again after a brief moment.
Try to remember the colors and their positions to make successful matches. Continue revealing and matching pairs until all tiles are revealed.
Once all pairs are matched, a message will pop up saying "You win! Refresh to start again."
